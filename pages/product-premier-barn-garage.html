Premier Barn Garage

The Premier Barn Garage blends classic barn styling with garage functionality. Built on the Premier series platform, it offers tall sidewalls, a sturdy floor system and options for garage doors, lofts and windows — great for vehicles, equipment storage or a workshop.

  • Barn roof style with 5/12 pitch and dimensional shingles
  • Approximately 6'8" clear sidewall height (varies by model)
  • LP SilverTech / SmartSide siding, 2×4 studs at 16" on center (model-dependent)
  • Options for overhead garage door, side personnel door, windows and loft
  • Engineered floor and reinforced framing for vehicle loads
