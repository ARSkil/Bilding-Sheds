Premier PRO Tall Barn

A high-capacity barn-style shed from the PRO Series, delivering extra headroom and storage versatility. Ideal for lofts, tall shelving, or larger equipment needing added vertical space.

  • Barn style roof with 5/12 pitch & 3-tab shingles
  • Approx. 7′10″ clear sidewall height for more vertical storage
  • LP SmartSide® siding; 2×4 studs spaced 16″ on center
  • Heavy-duty steel-reinforced door set; residential-grade hardware
  • Engineered floor system; soffited eaves and continuous ridge ventilation
